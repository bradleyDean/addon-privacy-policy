What happens when you request deletion

  • Your user account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your profile information will be removed.
  • Routes, comments, and other user-generated content associated with your account will be deleted.
  • Personal data stored in our systems will be removed.

Data retention

Some data may remain in the system for up to 30 days before being permanently deleted. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
