Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and display name.
  • User-generated content: routes you create, photos you upload, and comments you post.
  • Activity data: your climbing attempts, sends, and related performance stats.
  • Device & diagnostic data: app-usage analytics and crash reports collected via Firebase (Google).

How We Use Information

We use this information to operate the app, authenticate your account, provide functionality such as route sharing and performance tracking, and improve reliability.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Firebase for authentication, database storage, analytics, and crash reporting. These services may process data as described in Google’s privacy policy.

Deleting Your Account

You can permanently delete your account at any time from inside the app: go to Profile → Delete Account and follow the prompt. See our Account Deletion page for full details.

When you delete your account, your account, profile, comments, and activity data are removed. Routes you created are kept but anonymized — the author is shown as “Anonymous” — so other climbers’ shared lists continue to work.

Data Retention

We retain user data as long as necessary to provide the app’s services. After account deletion, residual copies may persist in backups for up to 30 days before permanent removal. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@addonclimbing.com (for account-deletion requests, see the Account Deletion page).

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
